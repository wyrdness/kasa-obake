## Cultural Sensitivity Note

When researching kasa-obake and tsukumogami:
- These represent living cultural values about respect and waste
- Understanding mottainai philosophy is essential context
- Shinto animistic beliefs underlie the folklore
- Popular "cute" portrayals should not overshadow deeper meaning
- Traditional values have contemporary relevance
